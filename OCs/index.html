• kit - main art by guro

    kit is my true self. pup's a small purple plushie who is a combination of a kitten, puppy, bunny, and mouse all in one. kits fur has blue spots, and a pink patch on the stomach. kit has a light and dark version, which i see as representations of different parts of myself. pup's blood and stuffing are a pearlescent blue colour, which can be seen if kit's fabric is torn. luckily, pup can be sewn back up to heal any wounds.

    kit's design is still actively developing, some ideas as of right now are fabric patches, and a scut tail.
