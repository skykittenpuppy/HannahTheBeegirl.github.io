main labels

  • demigirl
    one is partially, but not fully, a girl/woman. they may or may not identify as another gender in addition to being partially a girl

  • demifluid
    one's gender is partially genderfluid with the other part(s) of one's gender being static

  • acespike
    someone who usually feels no sexual attraction, but occasionally has rare, sudden, and intense spikes of sexual attraction for a short amount of time

  • nebularomantic
    unable to distinguish the difference between romantic and platonic attraction ... due to one's status as neurodivergent
  • alterous
    alterous attraction is an attraction and desire for an emotional closeness with a person that exists inbetween romantic and platonic feelings

  • polyamorous
    the capability or desire to be in a relationship with more than one individual at once
